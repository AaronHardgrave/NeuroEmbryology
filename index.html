Learning Objectives

Neural Tube Defects

  • Occulta: Malformed vertebrae
  • Meningocele: Membranes protrude through vertebral defect
  • Meningomyelocele: Neural tissue exposed through spinal opening

Formation of Brain Vesicles and Ventricles

Cephalic end of neural tube dilates, forming the 3 primary brain vesicles: prosencephalon (forebrain), mesencephalon (midbrain), rhombencephalon (hindbrain).
